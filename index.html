Super Hi!


My name is Yuri. I am software engineer and media artist from Moscow.
I graduated from Moscow State University, Department of Computational Mathematics and Cybernetics in 2010.

Programming allows me to bring great ideas to life, to build a world that I can share.

Currently I'm developing games, interactive installations and VR experiences using Unreal Engine, Qt/QML, Touch Designer, OpenFrameworks and other tools.

Feel free to contact me at yurikblech@gmail.com
