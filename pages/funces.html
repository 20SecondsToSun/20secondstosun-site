Funces

In May 2013 we got the task of creating an interactive booth for the advertising agency ADV event. We have developed a program that replaced heads of visitors to one of the eight 3d heads of known and unknown characters.

Kinect read out head model, leap motion controlled the stand.

At first, a welcome screen suggested to come closer (the approximate distance to the rack).




Then a small training instruction came. 3D heads were changing by leap motion swipe gesture, or you could have hidden you face by hands for the same result.




You could also change the size of the head from miniature to a hyperbolically huge by putting hands closer or further from each other.




Photos were made by fixing two fingers over the leap motion. After that photo automatically was sent to the server, qr-code and a direct link to the photo were generated.
