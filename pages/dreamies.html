Dreamies

An Interactive gaming booth for a cat food producer Dreamies was created in autumn 2014. You had to repeat one of the suggested poses on the screen. In case of success the comics around the user was drawn. It delineated the situation which the user was experiencing. After that the photo was taken. Player could have send it via email or post it in one of the social networks. System combined the depth map of kinect with rgb image of photo camera.

Scope: 6 booths were situated in 5 cities. Each weekend it was the new shopping center. Cities: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Yekaterinburg, Rostov-On-Don.
