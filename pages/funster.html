This application is an adaptation of another board game — Activity. Participants split to teams and try to explain words from cards to each other. Winning team is the one which does it more successfully. See the detail description of the game with screenshoots below





So. Game has loaded. Choose your native language or maybe language that you want to learn. In this case game will also be usefull




Adjust the level of difficulty, duration of one game, size of a board, sounds and make up teams




We have created several pretty avatars for you



You can also read detailed rules in the app




So, the game has started. A player chose a card and one word from it (each has its own level of difficulty, it influences onhow far a pawn will go if a player can explain it). There were approhimately 2000 words in two languages in the base, and we did it manually:)




Player explains while time runs out, also sometimes he can draw instead. We made a simple paint app for this




In the end players have result with a detailed statistics
