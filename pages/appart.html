Appart is a new look at what should be the picture in the interior today. A minimalistic digital frame, dynamic abstract art, dozens of works in one frame. Appart is a detail of the interior that attracts the eye. It looks both technologically and strictly. Harmoniously complement your space.

The app for iOS and Android syncs with your Appart and allows you to manage content in real time - include and turn off, add new pictures, switch art works.
