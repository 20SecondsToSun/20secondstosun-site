Casa Brutale

Casa Brutale is a conceptual home designed by OPA Open Platform from Architecture , and it's meant to be built within a cliff on an Aegean Island.

Its minimalism, elegance and atmosphere inspired me to create this architectural visualization and virtual reality experience.

I am convinced that this way is one of the best for showing arch concepts. It creates an opportunity for people to immerse into your own vision. Hope you'll enjoy.
