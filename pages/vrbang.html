VR Bang Game

Cute whale, rapid missile, donut, a piece of ham, cake, porcelain cat, everything smashed to small flinders only if looked at.

VR Bang is an arcade shooter designed specially for virtual reality.

Smash everything that flies, catch the clock, avoid the bombs, keep playing as long as possible! Bang! Bang! Bang!

Everything was made in Unreal Engine 4
