Jagermeister Bot

Before Halloween 2017 with the help of a chat-bot Jagermeistrer played the legendary bitter, tickets for parties and halloween-masks. Users installed telegram-bot @jagerhalloween_bot, sent their photo, which was processed using neural networks.A couple of moments and you have in the photo appeared jagermeister horns in the halloween-style.

The result was put on the participants in Instagram with a hashtag #scared_ice and participated in the drawing. The promotion was held in Vkontakte, Instagram and Telegram





Technologies

  • Qt/QML
  • Face++
  • Telegram API
