This stand translates last Moscow news in real time. Photos, videos, articles, schedule of the current and future events in the city, map of Moscow with all daily accidents - all of this day stuff you can view on the screen. You can manage content, save important news standing five feet from the booth and using kinect gestures or iPad, synchronized with a stand. All of this will be installed this year to the offices of Moscow media and to the halls of the negotiation rooms of large state companies in Moscow.

Video presentation UI

Interface. One of the main screens of the system displays. Depending on the number of raised fingers: one finger — all the news, two — news selected by theme. Circled in the air V symbol opens a materials faved by user. Raised hand calls a menu, gripped fist, making a circle in the air, calls material filters.







IPad application

You can find news and events in application by tag, text, date and using filters. Found material can be sent to the stand - simply select the new and snap it into the stand direction, just in a second card will appear at the screen. You can also customize the display of content on the home screen, control section of favorite materials and live from Moscow24.
